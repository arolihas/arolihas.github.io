A bunch of new things I am starting

August 7, 2020

In short: I accepted a TA position for OMSCS Deep Learning, extended my IBM internship to part-time this Fall, and joined the GT Academic Quizbowl Team!

Working remotely from home over the summer has been a challenge, but I am excited to start the semester with a lot of new things to look forward to. I expressed my interest in assisting the class over the summer after I took Deep Learning in the spring, but I got no response. Luckily I heard back much later with an interview and the offer to TA for the online masters’ course. As a Graduate Teaching Assistant, I get a stipend and a full tuition scholarship, so this was quite a relief!

I’ve also been learning a lot at my internship with IBM, and now that my manager has taken on a bigger project in the company, I was fortunate to tag along and continue my learning journey. As the company works to upgrade their cloud platforms, I will be working on the DevOps team to build and alerting and monitoring system from the ground up, picking up SeleniumJS, Salesforce, NewRelic, and PagerDuty along the way.

Since all of my classes and most of GT will be operating remotely, I decided to explore a new club to get some social interaction. I guess it’s not new, since I’ll be going back to my roots, as I did quiz bowl in high school. But I think it will be a lot of fun to jump back in, as I have been enjoying a lot of Jeopardy! and University Challenge during the pandemic.

Apologies for any perceived bragging, I’m not one to stunt but the past couple of years have been kind of rough, and I finally feel like things are starting to go back on the right track. With this new website, I’m starting a new chapter in my life, and I can’t wait to see what’s in store.
